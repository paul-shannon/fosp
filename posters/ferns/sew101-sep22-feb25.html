Disease Progression on the Windfall Trail

This formerly pure, lush stand lost a few ferns in 2021.

We installed a photograph station in 2022 to track the changes, inviting all passers-by to contribute photos.

Here we show the scene in September 2022, when the die-off was well begun, cross-fading into the scene in February 2025.

The die-off continues, moving up the slope at about 25 feet per year - and also now spreading to the west, across the sqebeqsed trail.
